# Benefits of Technology – Worksheets (Grade 4, Week 38)

## For the teacher

- **Use:** Use these worksheets **whenever** the topic of benefits of technology fits your curriculum. They are self-contained learning activities, not tied to a specific computer project.
- **Format:** Students work **offline**, with **pen and paper** (and color pencils if you like). No computer or internet needed.
- **Time:** About **25 minutes** per worksheet. Each part has a suggested time (roughly 3–6 minutes) so students can pace themselves.
- **Guidance:** The text is written so that students can complete it **on their own**; no teacher guidance is required during the activity.
- **Standalone value:** Each worksheet teaches on its own. The two sheets are **independent**—either can be used first in the same week. No computer project is required.

## Learning objectives

### Technical worksheet

- Explain a **benefit** as a helpful result that technology gives people.
- Name benefit types: **learn**, **communicate**, **save time / get things done**, and **stay safe or healthy**.
- Compare **before and after** when technology helps a school task.
- Choose the **right tool** for the benefit needed (not always the flashiest option).
- Recognize that benefits need **power**, **care**, and **wise use**.

### Social worksheet

- Explain that technology’s benefits affect **people** and communities, not only machines.
- Suggest ways to **share** technology benefits fairly when tools are shared.
- Name **social benefits** that connect people (inclusion, closeness, teamwork).
- Notice when not everyone has the **same access** to devices or internet.
- Use helpful technology with **kindness**, **permission**, and **balance**.
